Welcome to the California Country Club!

An Exclusive Private Country Club in Whittier California

Escape To Your Haven

California Country Club or CCC as it's affectionately known is an exclusive private golf club located in Whittier, a suburb of Los Angeles and only minutes from downtown LA making it extremely convenient. Experience breathtaking views while playing our 18-hole championship golf course , designed by famed golf course architect William Bell Jr. who also designed a little course down south called Torrey Pines. Our memberships have been designed with you and your lifestyle in mind. Take advantage of this hidden gem in the heart of SoCal and join us today.
